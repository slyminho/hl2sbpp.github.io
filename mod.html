Addons

Half-Life 2: Sandbox++ supports a wide variety of addons created by both the community and the developers.

Each addon adds something unique to the game — from new gameplay mechanics to visual customization.

Addons include Lua mods, playermodels, viewmodel hands, touchscreen configs, maps, and more.

Lua Modding

Lua modding is one of the most popular ways to expand the game.

Using Lua, you can create custom weapons, entities, props, and gameplay systems.

NPCs (Entities)

NPCs are a subcategory of Lua modding and require scripting to function properly.

You can create almost any type of NPC, as long as it is supported by the Lua API.

  • Enemies
  • Friendly characters
  • Interactive entities

Playermodels

Playermodels define how your character looks in-game.

They can be used for both players and NPCs (if properly configured).

Custom playermodels are a great way to personalize your experience and stand out.

Viewmodel Hands

Viewmodel hands are the hands you see in first-person view.

These are separate from your playermodel and only visible to you.

You can install custom hand models with different textures and styles.

Maps

Maps are the environments where gameplay takes place.

They can include complex worlds filled with props, entities, and scripted events.

  • Sandbox maps
  • Adventure maps
  • Custom environments
