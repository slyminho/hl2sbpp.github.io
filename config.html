Making your Half-Life 2: Sandbox++ game better

Default HL2:SB++ gameplay doesn't look very nice for new players.

You can install helpful addons and touchscreen configs (especially on Android).

Many useful addons can be found on the HL2:SB++ server.

Touch configs

If you are playing on Android, it's recommended to use community touchscreen layouts.

  • hahayoutookind touch
  • Steam Link touch
  • hl2sbpp_touch

You can find more configs in the "addons" section of the HL2:SB++ Discord. Choose what fits your playstyle best.

Configuring game settings

Adjusting settings is essential to improve your experience.

Change video settings at:
Settings → Video

Change graphics settings at:
Settings → Video → Graphics

PC players can adjust controls at:
Settings → Controls

To enable gamepad:
Settings → Controls → Advanced → Enable Gamepad

Personalization

Customize your identity in-game.

You can access these options through the "Advanced Settings" button.

Inside, you can:

  • Change your player name
  • Select a playermodel
  • Adjust colors
  • Preview your character in 3D
  • Change viewmodel hands

The "Player name" field defines how others see you in-game.

You can select from available models or import Garry's Mod playermodels.

The color buttons allow you to customize:

  • Playermodel color
  • Physgun light color

You can use presets or create your own colors.

You can also choose different viewmodel hands or download new ones online.
