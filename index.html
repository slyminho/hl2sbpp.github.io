What is Half-Life 2: Sandbox++?

Half-Life 2: Sandbox++ (HL2:SB++) is a remake of Half-Life 2: Sandbox, available for Android, Linux and Windows computers, using a modified Source Engine by nillerusr.

Lua Modding is supported, meaning you can create cool mods, just like in Garry's Mod.

Features

Half-Life 2: Sandbox++ has a bunch of features in-game. There is too many features, but here's the list of the main features:

  • Support for Lua Modding.
  • Stylized game like Garry's Mod.
  • Moddable game.

Community

Half-Life 2: Sandbox++ has a highly active and dedicated community that is always:

  • Helping new players get started
  • Playing together
  • Creating and porting new addons
  • Discussing ideas, updates, and improvements

You can join the community by entering their Discord Server.

Compatibility

Half-Life 2: Sandbox++ is highly compatible with a wide range of devices and systems, and can even run on low-end devices (with reduced graphics + the videoconfig patch).

Half-Life 2: Sandbox++ also includes Linux, Windows and MacOS versions of the game.
That means you can play Half-Life 2: Sandbox++ on Linux/Windows/MacOS computers along with Android devices.
